Industrial Products

AIJAZ Industries (Pvt) Ltd. is now a leading name in the field of retail and industries for its shelving, racking and display systems. It has reached this status by providing quality product and after sales services. This has not been achieved in days or months; behind this is a thoroughly devoted team having more than 40 years of experience.

Our transparent business deals and prompt response to the queries of our clients have gained us customer satisfaction and reputation in the market.

The furniture and equipment’s are locally manufactured at our factory. We have our own design and manufacturing facilities on the premises which allow parts to be made and/or altered quickly. Leveraging on our state-of-the-art infrastructure and a qualified and capable team of professionals, we have been providing our clients with complete display solution complying international quality standards. Our Uprights and Beams for Industrial Racking are manufactured through fully automated roll forming production lines. We are the pioneers in establishing such production facility in Pakistan.

We not only make customized racks but our racking and shelving is also trailer made, which means the proper use of space.

These are the salient features of our products • Quality design • Maximum storage space • Durability • Quality finishing • Multi-purpose functionality • Easy to install • Minimum maintenance required

Our products work well with • Supermarkets • Retail showrooms • Display centers • Warehouses Moreover, to provide complete assistance to our clients, we also provide installation of these display and storage racks at client's site.

Racking Features

Speed Lock Features

Speed lock pallet racking system is the most technologically advanced pallet storage system specifically designed to accommodate the latest changes and amendments to the international racking codes.

Wide/ Narrow Aisle Pallet Rack

Pallet racks are the primary storage component of most warehouses. Appropriate for both light duty and heavy duty applications, a well designed rack storage system is dynamic and reduces the overall cost of handling product in your organization

Double Deep Pallet Rack

Double Deep is similar to selective racking; but pallets are now stored two rows deep instead of one. This type of racking therefore requires specialized pallet handling equipment using ether a specialized forklift or a standard unit a with double deep handling attachment.

Long Span Shelving

The optimal system for manually storing small to medium-size products,its adaptability to any type of unit load; selective, as it allows immediate access to all unit loads and/or references stored; and dynamic, since it is easily assembled and/or disassembled, and components are also easily replaced and/or added.

Mezzanine Floor Racking

A Mezzanine floor is a raised platform that is supported by racking columns and is totally independent of the host building. It allows you to create additional floor space from wasted air space above existing work and storage areas and can be used to create storage.

Slotted Angle Racking

Used widely in offices, factories, libraries, kitchens, etc., for stacking files or bulk documents and in kitchens for placing and stacking utensils.
